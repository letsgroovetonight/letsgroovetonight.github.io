ABOUT US


Who we are, and what we do...


Boasting an all-star line up of AIM tertiary-trained musicians, and fronted by two highly-esteemed vocalists ('The Voice's' Chris Ninni and Jessica Lingotti) Let's Groove Tonight specialise in performing classic and current party hits, delivered with a R'n'B flavour. Featuring everything from Stevie Wonder's "Signed Sealed Delivered" to Daft Punk's "Get Lucky", the band have a library of over 250 songs, spanning from the 1960's to current Top 40s. 


This dynamic repertoire is matched with an explosive on-stage presence, honed through years of experience performing at such high-profile venues as Acer Arena, The Sydney Opera House and Telstra Stadium.  This experience has lead them to perform at events for clients including Microsoft, Ernst and Young, Nestle, Visa, Macquarie Bank and Holden, as well as literally hundreds of weddings across the country.


Combining virtuosity and talent with an exceptional on-stage presence, Let's Groove Tonight will create an entertainment experience that you won't forget! 


Let's Groove Tonight are available in the following configurations:


  • ﻿4-piece: Male Vocals, Guitar, Bass & Drums

  • 5-piece: Male Vocals, Female Vocals, Guitar, Bass & Drums

  • 6-piece: Male Vocals, Female Vocals, Keyboard, Guitar, Bass & Drums
