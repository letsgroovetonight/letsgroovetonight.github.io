Weddings


The perfect music for the biggest party of your life!


Understanding that every wedding is a unique event, we offer flexible performances tailored to suit your vision. Drawing from an enormous and eclectic repertoire, spanning jazz and lounge standards to dance floor favourites, LGT are a proven hit with all ages and tastes. 


By providing a complete package (including full PA, lighting, sound engineer, wireless microphone for speeches and unlimited song requests), we ensure that all the little details of the night's entertainment are taken care of, and your wedding is transformed from good to unforgettable.

Corporate Events


Impress with the best.


LGT are the go-to band for Sydney's corporate elite, boasting a list of past high-profile clients that includes IBM, Ernst & Young, Visa, Microsoft, Macquarie Bank, Nestle and Holden. As the band is comprised entirely of professionally trained musicians, who have toured and played with artists such as Bon Jovi, Lionel Ritchie, Jimmy Barnes and Shannon Noll, we are renowned for our efficient and professional conduct. This asset, coupled with their dynamic, crowd-engaging performances, make LGT a foolproof choice for any corporate function.

Parties & Public Gigs


Keep the audience on their feet.


﻿﻿With extensive performance experience, including television appearances, stadium concerts, broadway shows and much more, LGT know how to entertain a crowd. Priding ourselves on our ability to get audiences on the dancefloor and keeping them there, we perform energetic, engaging and fast-paced shows that deliver something for everyone.
